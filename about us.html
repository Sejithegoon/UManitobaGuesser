About Us

We’re two Computer Science students at the University of Manitoba building this project to improve our skills and create something fun for the community. This is only the beginning we’ll be regularly updating the website with new features, better gameplay, and more campus locations. If you have ideas, requests, or anything you want to see added, join our Discord and let us know. We’re always listening to feedback and building based on what people want. We’re also looking for more developers who want to contribute and help grow the project. If you’re interested, don’t hesitate to reach out. This site is going to keep evolving more content, more features, and a better experience over time.
